Vikings, or Norsemen, refer to seafaring people from what is today the Nordic countries. The Viking Age is commonly referred to as from 793 AD to 1066 AD, during which Vikings had a profound impact on the early medieval history of Europe. Vikings were traders and navigated seas and rivers as far as Constantinople, the Middle East and even what is today America in their iconic long boats. However, the Vikings were perhaps more known for raiding villages and were infamous warriors and conquerors. Former Viking settlements are today important centers of trade, such as London, Dublin and Kiev. The Vikings spoke Norse, similar to today’s Icelandic, and wrote in runes. Until today, rune stones are present in the Nordic countries, telling the tales of our ancestors. The Vikings were pagans and worshipped multiple gods, including the now popularized Odin, Thor and Loki. Today, many old Viking traditions are still a part of life in the modern Nordic countries. At the Viking Party Manila, we celebrate our joint history and cultural ties in a fun and engaging way.

NORDCHAM PHILIPPINES

Established in 2012, the Nordic Chamber of Commerce of the Philippines (NordCham Philippines) connects the Nordic-Philippine business community and promotes trade, industry, and investment between the Philippines and the Nordic countries (Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway, and Sweden).

As the largest Nordic chamber in Asia with 130+ members, NordCham drives sustainability and supports diverse industries such as ICT, clean technologies, maritime, healthcare, and financial services through events, networking, and consulting services.

Key activities include the Sustainability Committee Meeting, Nordic Sustainability Awards, Innovation Exchange, and the Viking Party Manila, which celebrates Nordic heritage in a fun and engaging way.

The VIKING PARTY MANILA is arranged by NordCham Philippines as a tribute to our common Viking Heritage and a fun way to celebrate our Nordic roots.
