NORDCHAM PHILIPPINES

Established in 2012, the Nordic Chamber of Commerce of the Philippines (NordCham Philippines) connects the Nordic-Philippine business community and promotes trade, industry, and investment between the Philippines and the Nordic countries (Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway, and Sweden).

As the largest Nordic chamber in Asia with 130+ members, NordCham drives sustainability and supports diverse industries such as ICT, clean technologies, maritime, healthcare, and financial services through events, networking, and consulting services.

Key activities include the Sustainability Committee Meeting, Nordic Sustainability Awards, Innovation Exchange, and the Viking Party Manila, which celebrates Nordic heritage in a fun and engaging way.

The VIKING PARTY MANILA is arranged by NordCham Philippines as a tribute to our common Viking Heritage and a fun way to celebrate our Nordic roots.
