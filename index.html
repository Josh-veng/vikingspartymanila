VIKING PARTY MANILA

The Viking Party Manila is a one of a kind evening of fun, food, music and dance, arranged by NordCham Philippines in celebration of our Nordic heritage and our Viking roots. The event is a casual party to which anyone is welcome. Come dressed up in your favorite Viking attire or come as you are. Party program includes Viking-themed entertainment from stage, live music, rich servings of great Nordic food and free-flowing beers of highest quality and quantity.

EAT AND DRINK LIKE A VIKING

Indulge in a feast of delicatessen from the Nordic countries! Dusit Hotel, one of the best culinary destinations in Manila, invites everyone to the taste of Nordic Food at a overflowing buffet table. For the real Valhalla experience, Engkanto Brewery is providing free-flowing beer all night long! Engkanto is known to brew one of the best beers in the Philippines. Unlimited beer is included in the Viking Party Pass. A selection of fine Wines and Mixed Drinks will be available at cash bars around the event area.

PARTY LIKE A VIKING

Participate in the endless cheers and sing along with the music! And for those ready to rave – our DJ will take down the house on a pulsating dancefloor until midnight.

NORDCHAM PHILIPPINES

Established in 2012, the Nordic Chamber of Commerce of the Philippines (NordCham Philippines) connects the Nordic-Philippine business community and promotes trade, industry, and investment between the Philippines and the Nordic countries (Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway, and Sweden).

As the largest Nordic chamber in Asia with 130+ members, NordCham drives sustainability and supports diverse industries such as ICT, clean technologies, maritime, healthcare, and financial services through events, networking, and consulting services.

Key activities include the Sustainability Committee Meeting, Nordic Sustainability Awards, Innovation Exchange, and the Viking Party Manila, which celebrates Nordic heritage in a fun and engaging way.

The VIKING PARTY MANILA is arranged by NordCham Philippines as a tribute to our common Viking Heritage and a fun way to celebrate our Nordic roots.
